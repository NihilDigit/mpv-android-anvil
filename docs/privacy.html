Privacy Policy

This privacy policy pertains to the ANVIL VFI Demo app.

ANVIL VFI Demo does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. The app has no user accounts, no analytics, no telemetry, and no advertising. All video processing is performed locally on-device.

The app does not access the network except when the user explicitly opens a network stream URL for video playback. No data is sent from the app to any server.

Changes to this Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy is effective as of March 2026 and will remain in effect except with respect to any changes in its provisions in the future, which will be in effect immediately after being posted on this page.
